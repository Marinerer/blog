I've wanted to build a photography gallery site for a long time, but never had the inspiration until I found [Afilmory](https://github.com/Afilmory/afilmory). I really like its web UI design, so I spent a few weeks building my own site [Lumine](https://gallery.shiyu.me/) to show photos from my collection.

It turned out simple and clean. Free services like Cloudflare R2 and Vercel are enough for me.
